Chapter 11

Key Takeaways

🧠

LSTM Forgets

LSTMs process sequences step-by-step, causing early context to fade. The further away a word is, the harder it is to remember — making long sentences a real challenge.

👁️

Attention Sees All

Attention lets the model look at every word simultaneously, weighting each by relevance. No information is lost to distance.

🚀

Foundation of Modern NLP

This mechanism is the backbone of BERT, GPT, and all Transformers. You've just learned the core idea behind the AI revolution.
